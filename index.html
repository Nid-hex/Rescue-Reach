Compassion in Action

GIVING VOICELESS ANIMALS A SECOND CHANCE AT LIFE

Animal Matter To Me (AMTM) is a non-profit organization dedicated to the rescue, care, and rehabilitation of injured, stray, and abandoned animals across India. Since our inception, we have been at the forefront of providing medical assistance, shelter, and lifelong support to animals who have no other means of survival. From neutering to vaccination drives and emergency rescue operations, our team is committed to ensuring the welfare and dignity of animals that have no voice of their own. Every animal we save is a life that matters to us.

Bruno – A Brave Rescue Story

Bruno was found injured near the highway and was rescued by our team...

Bruno was found with a broken leg near the highway. Our team immediately brought him to the shelter, gave him medical care, and lots of love. Today, he is healthy, playful, and ready for adoption. He loves to cuddle, enjoys long walks, and trusts humans again.

Misty – The Sweetest Kitty

Misty was abandoned but she never stopped trusting people...

Misty was found near a market area. She was weak and hungry. After joining our shelter, she recovered quickly and became the most affectionate cat we’ve ever met. She is gentle, friendly, and loves warm blankets.
